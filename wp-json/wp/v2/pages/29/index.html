Brief Bio<\/h3>\n

Lynn Mehta is an award-winning Virginia-based artist known for her fluid and expressive oil paintings<\/span>. Her art reflects a deep connection to the ever-changing visual world, blending abstract shapes and forms inspired by a diversity of subject matter. Her paintings, rich in texture and depth, blend spontaneity with control, captivating viewers both up close and from afar.<\/span><\/p>\n

Growing up along the Pacific Ocean, Mehta has been an artist as long as she can remember and was inspired by the work and encouragement of her mother an artist herself. Mehta earned her B.A. in Art from San Diego State University, and has studied with an impressive list of\u00a0 acclaimed artists in the US and abroad.<\/p>\n

Mehta is a Signature Member of the American Impressionist Society and the American Society of Marine Artists. She is featured in galleries, private and public collections, books, and magazines worldwide, including the Maryhill Museum of Art in Washington, the National Institutes of Health, and Acadia National Park Artists-in-Residency.<\/span><\/p>\n
