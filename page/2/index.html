Welcome to Lynn Mehta Fine Art

"Hello, I am Lynn Mehta and the paintings I create are the art of Impressionism. It is important for me to give you the surprise of discovery when you look closely at my painting and then step back, allowing room for your imagination. I work and paint in my home studio in Alexandria, Virginia. When I paint indoors I rely on my inspiration from the scene and my outdoor sketches to create a painting that is real to me."
